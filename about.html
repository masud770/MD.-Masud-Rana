About Me

I am a dedicated and versatile professional in the field of Computer Science and Engineering, with extensive expertise in machine learning, natural language processing (NLP), computer vision, big data, and IoT. My academic and professional journey is underscored by a strong foundation in programming, software development, and research, with a particular focus on cutting-edge technologies and their real-world applications. As the Vice President of the NLP Lab student forum 2024-25 at Daffodil International University, I have had the privilege to lead impactful research initiatives, mentor aspiring technologists, and contribute to advancing innovation in the field. My thesis research on answering Bangla factoid questions using parameter-efficient fine-tuned LLMs reflects my commitment to solving complex challenges and advancing localized AI solutions. My areas of expertise extend to fine-tuning large language models (LLMs), sequence-to-sequence models, and pervasive computing, which I apply in both academic and industrial contexts. I am particularly interested in the intersection of machine learning and real-world applications, as demonstrated through my leadership in graphics engineering projects, such as creating a "Shadhinotar" (Independence) scenery of Bangladesh and delivering expert presentations on topics like ray tracing. In addition to research and development, I actively engage in community-driven activities, including participation in hackathons, workshops, and content creation through my Masud Rana YouTube channel, where I aim to inspire and educate fellow enthusiasts in the tech domain. Looking ahead, I aspire to establish a software development company that bridges emerging technologies with practical applications, particularly in the domains of cybersecurity, machine learning, and blockchain. With a solution-driven mindset, a strong work ethic, and a commitment to lifelong learning, I am dedicated to making meaningful contributions to the field of computer science and engineering.
